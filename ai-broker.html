Flyhomes AI Broker

An AI chat feature designed to give home buyers instant, accurate answers to complex property questions — directly inside the search flow.

CompanyFlyhomes
RoleProduct Designer
ToolFigma
Timeline3 Months

Overview

An online AI chat interface built to improve the home‑purchasing experience.

Flyhomes AI Broker lets buyers ask questions in plain language and get instant, research‑backed answers — without leaving the search flow.

“What should I look out for with this home?”

The AI Broker conducts home research automatically. Buyers review the items it surfaces inside an AI‑generated report.

“Is this home close to high‑voltage power lines?”

Buyers ask specific, situational questions about a property and receive immediate, context‑aware responses.

“Show me a comparable analysis.”

The AI Broker identifies recently sold, similar nearby homes — helping buyers understand the pricing context.

The Challenge

New agent‑commission rules intensify market competition.

A regulatory change shifts responsibility for the buyer's‑agent commission onto purchasers, with fees becoming negotiable rather than a standardized percentage. Buyers grew more cost‑conscious, and brokerages had to prove their value in a far more competitive market.

Research

Understanding the buyer, and where Flyhomes could win.

Problem Statement

How might we provide better service at a lower cost, while standing out in an increasingly competitive market?

The Solution

A new product: Flyhomes AI Broker.

Align business and user goals with AI.

Rather than add another tool to the agent, we put an AI broker directly in the buyer's hands — automating research and answering questions instantly. It lowers the cost of service for Flyhomes while giving buyers faster, more confident decisions.

Process

How can we help buyers with AI?

01

Map the home‑buying journey

We analyzed the full buying journey end to end, identifying the key touchpoints where AI could enhance — or fully automate — the help a buyer needs.

02

Prioritize features

Client and agent interviews surfaced the AI capabilities experienced buyers valued most. Working with engineering, data, and legal, we assessed feasibility and risk to prioritize features across four development phases.

Design Iteration

Refining the experience through testing.

Iteration 1

A more compact layout, less scrolling

The card‑based layout was replaced with a condensed, email‑inbox‑style list view — reducing navigation demands and making it faster to browse.

Iteration 2

Easier switching between properties

Testing showed buyers evaluate several homes at once. A dropdown selector in the address bar lets them switch property conversations in a single tap.

Iteration 3

A dedicated thread per call‑out item

The single‑thread model was replaced with one discussion thread per identified item, so users could stay focused and return to a specific context later.

Outcome

Increased 7‑day retention.

A limited‑feature version launched inside the existing app. Buyers who engaged with the AI Chat feature showed meaningfully higher retention than those who didn't.
